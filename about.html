About Our Institute

Nurturing Classical Music Excellence Since 2023

Our Legacy

Aditi Institute of Sursadhanolay is more than an academy; it is a movement dedicated to preserving and evolving Indian classical arts in Murshidabad. Our legacy is defined by excellence and accessible, high-quality cultural education.

A Cultural Hub for Classical Arts

We serve as a center for classical music education, instilling discipline and technical mastery. By focusing on traditional forms, we ensure Indian musical heritage continues to thrive.

From Jiaganj to the Global Stage

We connect local talent with global icons through workshops and performances led by renowned artists:

  • Purbayan Chatterjee – Sitar virtuoso
  • Makrand Deshpande – Actor & theatre director
  • Ojas Adhiya – Tabla maestro

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To provide holistic training in music, dance, and theatre while nurturing discipline, creativity, and long-term dedication to the arts.

👁️ Vision

To build a platform where young talent from Murshidabad can access world-class opportunities and perform on global stages.

✨ Values

Discipline (Sadhana), inclusivity, excellence, and preservation of cultural heritage while supporting modern artistic growth.

Why Choose Aditi Institute

🌟 Expert Faculty

Learn from renowned maestros with decades of experience in Indian classical music. Our faculty members are award-winning musicians and dedicated educators.

📚 Comprehensive Curriculum

From basic fundamentals to advanced techniques, our curriculum covers all aspects of classical music training including theory, practice, and performance.

🎤 Performance Opportunities

Regular concerts, recitals, and competitions provide our students with platforms to showcase their talent and gain real-world performance experience.

👁️ Personalized Attention

Small class sizes and one-on-one sessions ensure that each student receives customized guidance tailored to their individual pace and learning style.

🏛️ State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our academy is equipped with modern classrooms, practice rooms, and a traditional concert hall with excellent acoustics for optimal learning.

🌍 Cultural Heritage

We are dedicated to preserving authentic Indian classical music traditions while adapting to modern educational methodologies and contemporary needs.
