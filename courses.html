Our Courses

Comprehensive Music Training Programs for All Levels

Primary Courses

🎵 Hindustani Classical Vocal
Master the art of Hindustani vocal music including Khayal, Thumri, Dadra, and Bhajans. Learn proper breathing techniques, raag concepts, and performance skills.
⏰ 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM All Levels
🎸 Sitar & String Instruments
Learn to play sitar with proper fingering techniques, raag interpretation, and classical compositions. Also includes training in sarangi and other string instruments.
⏰ 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM All Levels
🎸 Guitar Basics & Advanced
Learn to play acoustic and electric guitar. Master chords, scales, fingerpicking techniques, and popular songs. Suitable for beginners to advanced players looking to improve their skills.
⏰ 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM All Levels
🪘Tabla, Cajon & Clap Box
Comprehensive training in drum playing for both accompaniment and solo performances. Learn raag progressions, scales, and melodic compositions.
⏰ 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM All Levels
🎹Piano & keyboard
Comprehensive keyboard training covering piano techniques, music theory, sight-reading, and repertoire. Learn to play classical pieces, contemporary music, and develop keyboard proficiency.
⏰ 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM All Levels
🎼 Classical and Hip Hop Training
Learn classical dance forms and modern hip hop choreography. Develop rhythm, coordination, and expressive movement. Includes traditional and contemporary dance techniques for performance and fitness.
⏰ 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM All Levels
🎭 Acting Theory & Notation
Study acting techniques, script analysis, character development, and performance theory. Learn stage presence, improvisation, and theatrical notation for aspiring actors and performers.
⏰ 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM All Levels
🥁Drums & Percussion
Master drum kit, percussion instruments, and rhythm techniques. Learn beats, fills, and ensemble playing. Covers various styles from rock to traditional percussion for comprehensive training.
⏰ 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM All Levels

Specialized Programs

📖 Music Theory & Notation
Understand the theoretical foundations of Indian classical music. Learn raag classification, taal structures, notation systems, and compositional techniques.
⏰ 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM Intermediate+
🎤 Performance & Stage Techniques
Master the art of stage performance, microphone handling, audience engagement, and concert presentation skills. Build confidence for public performances.
⏰ 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM Advanced
👥 Group Performance & Ensemble
Learn to perform in groups, understand ensemble dynamics, and develop collaborative skills. Experience the joy of collective music-making.
⏰ 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM All Levels
🎼 Composition & Improvisation
Learn to compose your own raags and bandishes. Master improvisation techniques and develop creativity within the framework of classical traditions.
⏰ 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM Intermediate+
🎓 Certification Programs
Complete structured courses with certification. Choose from Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced diploma programs. Recognized credentials for professional musicians.
⏰ Multiple Schedules All Levels
👶 Children's Music Classes
Special programs designed for children aged 5-12. Fun, interactive learning with games and activities to develop musical interest and talent.
⏰ 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Kids Only
