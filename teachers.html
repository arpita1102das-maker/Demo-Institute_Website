Rahul Dev Ghosh

🎭 Acting Theory & Notation

Rahul Dev Ghosh is a seasoned theatre director, actor, and educator with 36+ years of experience in the performing arts.
He specializes in stagecraft, creative direction, and acting, and has led over 30 major productions. He is also a dedicated mentor, actively training students through theatre workshops since 1991.

Subhra Sankha Sarkar

🪘Tabla, Cajon & Clap Box

Subhra Sankha Sarkar is a talented young tabla artist from Kolkata, trained under renowned गुरु Pandit Subhajyoti Guha.
Known for his dedication and skill, he has won multiple prestigious awards, including Dover Lane (2013) and Rajya Sangeet Academy (2014). He is also a National Scholarship holder from CCRT and a multi-time university festival champion.

Saurav A Gangulii

🪕 Sitar & String Instruments

Saurav A Gangulii is a skilled sitar artist from a musical family, trained at ITC Sangeet Research Academy under renowned maestros like Pandit Buddhadeb Das Gupta and Pandit Manilal Nag.
An award-winning musician, he has contributed to projects like Chandrayaan-3's anthem and Bollywood film Kesari Veer. He is also an AIR-graded artist and a recipient of national scholarships in classical music.
